Woodwork

Lee

Whether creating fabulous summer beers, old-school EPAs or crisp lagers, YSB co-founder and chief architect Lee finds ways to blend the new and exotic with the traditional and classic, which is not only a metaphor for keeping Colin and Dave entertained. With additions such as ginger, cacoa nibs, lychees, rice and more, his riffs on tried-and-tested recipes have been known to raise the roof on more than one occasion. Often suprising, always ambrosial, his creations make sure we're zinging all summer long, and then cozy while we hibernate through the British winter. Characteristically understated, Lee let's his beer do the talking, and it usually says, "Oh go on then, just one more" - so get it while you can!
