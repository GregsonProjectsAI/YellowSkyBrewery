Chris

Co-founder Chris has developed many beers of all different styles - everything from low ABV table beer through to the portliest of pokey porters, intense IPAs and more. His dedication to experimentation has produced some outstanding brews, and kept the beers fresh and exciting. Chris has spent a lot of time turning Yellow Sky from a few disparate beers brewed by great mates to a coherent brand. His themed names, artistic direction, enthusiasm for the craft and readiness to get stuck in have led to interesting collaborations in the local community, most recently with Tilford Brewery and Owlrye, marking the first time that Yellow Sky recipes made it into pubs. This was a major and exciting milestone for all the boys, and especially The Landlord, Colin. In addition to that, Chris has recently achieved a Merit for The Brewlab Certificate in Practical Brewing, an excellent achievement and a massive stepping stone for the boys and the brewery.
