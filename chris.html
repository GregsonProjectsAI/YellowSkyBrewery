Chris

As one of the key brewers here at Yellow Sky, Chris has developed many beers of all different styles - everything from low ABV table beer through to the portliest of pokey porters, intense IPAs and more. His dedication to experimentation has produced some outstanding beers, and kept the beers fresh and exciting. Chris has spent a lot of time turning Yellow Sky from a few disparate beers brewed by great mates to a coherent brand. His themed names, artistic direction, enthusiasm for the craft and readiness to get stuck in have led to interesting collaborations in the local community, most recently with Tilford Brewery, marking the first time that Yellow Sky recipes made it into pubs, with Owl Rye. This was a major and exciting milestone for all the boys, and especially The Landlord, Colin. In addition to that, Chris has recently achieved a Merit for The Brewlab Certificate in Practical Brewing, an excellent achievement and a massive stepping stone for the boys and the brewery.
