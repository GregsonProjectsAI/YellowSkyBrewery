Dave

Dave is Chris's Dad. When he retired from Real Science, he was gifted by his work friends a Braumeister home brewing setup, which he, Chris and Lee practiced on in Dave's garage. That was almost 10 years ago. Now, after reading all the words there are, showing no mercy for speakers at educational events, and, frankly, brewing quite a few banging beers, he has taught the boys all he knows. These days, happy to be given a washing up brush, a sausage and the odd game of darts, he supports the boys in whatever way he can (translation: buys all the ingredients) to ensure great beer keeps flowing from the Yellow Sky. A pioneer, educator, and investor, Dave has earned his place as 2nd-least respected man at the brewery.
