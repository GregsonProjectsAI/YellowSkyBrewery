## How to Get the Best Out of It (Prompting Best Practices)

To get exactly what you want, try to follow these principles when you ask me to generate something:

- **Be Specific About Style**: Terms like *"glassmorphism"*, *"flat vector"*, *"neo-brutalism"*, *"photorealistic"*, or *"minimalist line art"* go a long way.
- **Specify the Colors**: Provide color names or vibes (e.g., *"neon purple and slate grey"*, *"warm earthy tones"*) to ensure it matches your brand's palette.
- **Set the Layout Rules**: For UI designs, clarify that you want *"no surrounding device frames"* so the image focuses purely on the interface. Detail the components you want to see (e.g., *"sidebar navigation, top search bar, masonry grid layout"*).
- **Iterate**: Don't worry if the first result isn't perfect. Tell me what to tweak, and we can adjust the prompt.
