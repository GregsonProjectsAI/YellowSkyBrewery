But First...

How three nitwits went from a garage to a pub tap.

01 / 04

In The Beginning...

It all started where most bad decisions begin: Dave's garage. Armed with a few plastic buckets, some stolen brewing kettles, and absolutely zero professional experience, we set out to make beer. Most of it was terrible. Some of it was dangerous. But we were hooked.

02 / 04

From Garage To Shed

When Dave's garage ran out of space (and patience), the operation was forced to relocate to Colin's garden shed. We considered this a massive upgrade. We finally had room to scale up our catastrophic experiments. Mistakes were still made. Explosions, however, were kept to a minimum.

03 / 04

Home Improvements

Eventually, we realized that if we were going to be proper brewers, we needed proper gear. We ripped out the old wood, brought in the shiny steel, and stopped guessing temperatures by sticking our fingers in the mash. Yellow Sky Brewery was officially born, and the beer stopped tasting like regret.

04 / 04

Into The Pub

The real test wasn't surviving the shed; it was surviving the public. We teamed up with the legends down in Tilford, and for the first time, a Yellow Sky brew flowed from a real pub tap. Seeing our quirky little birds perched on the bar... well, it almost made the garage fires worth it.
